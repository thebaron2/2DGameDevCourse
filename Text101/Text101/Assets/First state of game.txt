Start of CYOA game:
You wake up in a bed, not knowing where or who you are. 
You look out the window and realize that you are on spaceship going through an asteroid field.
As you realize this an alarm starts to go off, you are thinking one of the asteroids must have hit the ship.
You quickly get dressed and head to the door. When you open the door the alarm gets louder, you have to find out what is going on.

You can either A) go right towards the elevator or B) go left towards the emergency ladder.
